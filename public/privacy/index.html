What Riff Radar collects

Riff Radar logs the following to improve your experience:

  • Which songs you bring to Groove (title and artist)
  • The timestamps or moments you mark
  • Whether you clicked through to a music streaming service
  • Basic session activity — messages exchanged, recommendations generated

Riff Radar does not collect the actual words you write to Groove, or any personal information.

This data is never sold or shared with advertisers.
